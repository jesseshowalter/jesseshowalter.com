Hawaii Theatre Center Website

Hawaii Theatre is dedicated to providing a broad range of entertainment, cultural and educational experiences to benefit the people and visitors of Hawaii in a facility of recognized excellence. They were in need of a website that maintained that same level of excellence. They were also in need of a website that allowed them to dynamically maintain the activities that go on at the theatre.

  • Web Design
  • Web Development
  • Photography

From Drab to Fab

There old site was stuck in black and white. The aim was to bring them up to date with an invigorated design that catered to there user base. Allowing users to easily browse showtimes and tickets, while still allowing the theatre to be held in the regard of a landmark.
