totalliferecovery.com

Total Life Recovery Website

Total Life Recovery is a faith-based program where incarcerated women are provided the opportunity to transform into “Na Wahine O Kupono” – Women of Excellence and start their journey to becoming contributing members of society. I helped them raise much-needed awareness for this program by creating a website which explores the lives of four incarcerated women involved with the Total Life Recovery(TLR) Program. All Work was done while at Orangeroc under the creative direction of Melinda Mullis
