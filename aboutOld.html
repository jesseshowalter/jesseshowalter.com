Handsome Design & Code

Hi, I’m Jesse. I was born and raised in California’s Famous “Bay Area” before relocating to Oahu 8 years ago. I have always been a fan of puzzles and solving problems and love the challenges that visual communication & design affords me. Contact me if you would like to work with me.

I started out my career designing anything and everything. Although I was formally trained in traditional Print Design, I've had the privilege of branching out into the world of web design and specifically User Interface and User Experience Design. I enjoy mixing those basic design principles with the power of the web.
