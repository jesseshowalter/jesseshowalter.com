Sasquatch Shades Online Store

Sasquatch Shades needed an online store to sell there eco friendly sunglasses made from natural materials. It needed to match their go green attitude, and layed back style, letting the shades do the talking.

  • Web Design
  • E-Commerce
  • Web Development
  • Photography

Beautifully Simple

The simple idea of selling sunglasses made of recycled natural woods is beautiful in it's self. It need an online store that matched that ethos. The site is clean and un cluttered, allowing people to enjoy their shopping experience. Under the hood the site is no different, built on the lightweight CMS Perch and paired with the powerfull Foxycart, this site is built for ease.
