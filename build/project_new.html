Memorate

Memorate.com is an amazing new social network that empowers people to collaborate and share in eachothers stories, by co authoring stories with friends and family. They needed a fresh new look that would appeal to their target demographic while still staying relavent to all markets. I did a complete overhaul on there User Flow and Strategy while designing them a simple and stylized interface with and uncluttered and classic feel. Mario Quezada collaborated on design , Angela Neri assisted with Content Stratgey. All Work was done while at Orangeroc
